Varshitha Gunnala acts in a play

Author: Henrik Ibsen

MRS. ELVSTED.

Oh yes, I suppose I am.

HEDDA.

And how mortally tired you look.

MRS. ELVSTED.

Yes, I am mortally tired.

HEDDA

Well then, you must do as I tell you. You must go into my room and lie down for a little while.
